AI‑Eye

This project describes a wearable intelligent device for blind and visually impaired users. By using artificial intelligence, multiple cameras, and audio output, the system analyzes the surrounding environment from multiple angles and provides real-time spoken guidance.

The device is designed as lightweight smart glasses or a head-mounted frame equipped with multiple cameras (front, back, left, right, and limited vertical coverage) to maximize environmental awareness.

Key Features

  • Wearable, glasses-like, lightweight design
  • Multi-camera system with wide-angle coverage
  • AI-powered visual processing and scene understanding
  • Object, obstacle, person, text, and event detection
  • Natural, directional audio guidance via multiple speakers
  • Durable and impact-resistant construction
  • Upgradeable and extensible software architecture

The main goal of this invention is to improve independence, safety, and overall quality of life for blind users by providing meaningful, real-time understanding of their surroundings.
